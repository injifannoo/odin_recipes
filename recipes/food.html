Caccabsaa favorite food

caccabsaa is one of the well known oromo cultural food!

caccabsaa is eaten during some ceremony and holidays. And also for special guests!

Ingredients

  • teff
  • butter
  • other optinal ingredients like berber

Instructions

  1. make bread from teff/maxino
  2. cut down bread into small pieces
  3. mix pieces of bread wih butter
